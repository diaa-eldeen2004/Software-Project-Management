Knowledge without barriers.

ICDLS is a calm, cloud-based academic library where technology stays quiet and your work stays protected. Our goal is to connect students, researchers, librarians, and IT teams around the same shared collections.

How ICDLS helps

A single cloud-based library for institutions worldwide. Students discover readings, researchers share work, librarians shape collections, and IT teams keep everything safe in the background.
